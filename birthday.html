25th Birthday!!!

If you've reached this page (or looked at the source code), 1) you're invited to my 25th birthday party, and 2) thank you for being an awesome friend!

(tl;dr I'm having a race along Manhattan, followed by dinner! Please let me know of your availability here.)

I may have alluded to the mischief I have planned to you, but, officially: for my 25th birthday, I have planned a race along Manhattan, from the highest-numbered street (220th) to the lowest -- 1st St. (and 1st Ave.)!

(Sort of. The race is held in two parts, with the first leg ending at Marisa's apartment [address below] and the second leg at the listed location of 1st/1st. The rest stop is for snacks/warming up/bathrooms/more shenanigans.)

The exact mechanics of the game will be discussed at the start of the race, but here is the basic idea:

  • The island is divided into regions -- Inwood, Harlem, Central Park, etc. -- and locations -- the Met Museum and Lincoln Center.
  • Each region or location will have a site-specific quiz or activity associated with it. The activities will be shared at the start of the race, but can only be completed at the site; the quizzes will of course be hidden until participants request to answer the question.
  • There will also be Brian-related quizzes and New York-related activities that can be done at any time. These are somewhere between helpful and necessary to win the race.

That should be plenty preamble for now! The day's schedule is:

  • 1 P.M.: lunch at the Marble Hill Chick-Fil-A! Self-paid and optional; please join if you can!
  • 2 P.M.: the race begins!
  • 3:30 P.M.-ish: arrive at Marisa's apartment [return above] @ 200 W 79th St. #15N; the first winners are crowned!
  • 4:30 P.M.: part 2!
  • 6 P.M.-ish: arrive at 1st/1st; the second winners are crowned!
  • 6:30 P.M.: dinner at [TBD; most likely Italian nearby]; thanks for dinner Dad!
  • etc.

N.B.: in lieu of gifts, I ask that you fund your own subway fare (even though you have to answer quizzes and do activities to ride them :D). OMNY should cap everyone at $34 for the day ...

Please let me know of your availability here. Looking forward to seeing each and every one of you!
